🛡 VintedScorer

Privacy Policy — Politique de confidentialité

Last updated: April 2026

VintedScorer is a browser extension that analyses prices and detects scam signals on Vinted. We take your privacy seriously — this page explains exactly what data is used and how.

📦 Data we collect

We collect no personal data. VintedScorer does not collect, transmit, store or share any information about you or your browsing activity.

💾 Local storage only

The extension stores the following data locally on your device only, using chrome.storage.local:

  • Your language preference (FR or EN)
  • Hidden group preferences for the price clustering popup

This data never leaves your browser and is never sent to any server.

🌐 Network requests

VintedScorer makes the following network requests:

  • Vinted pages — the extension reads the content of Vinted pages you visit to analyse prices and descriptions. It does not send this content anywhere.
  • Vinted internal API (/api/v2/users/:id) — to fetch seller profile data (ratings, transaction history) already visible on Vinted. These requests use your existing Vinted session cookies and are made directly to Vinted's own servers.

No data from these requests is stored or transmitted to third parties.

🔒 Permissions explained

  • activeTab — to read the current Vinted page content for analysis.
  • tabs — to detect which Vinted domain you're on and update the extension icon.
  • storage — to save your language preference and group visibility settings locally.
  • Host permissions (vinted.*) — to activate the extension only on Vinted domains.

👤 Third parties

VintedScorer does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with any third party.

👶 Children's privacy

VintedScorer does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

📬 Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on the extension's support page or contact us via the Chrome Web Store listing.
